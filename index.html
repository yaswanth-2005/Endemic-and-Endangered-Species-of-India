The.,
Himalayas

The Himalayas are home to an estimated 10,000 plant species, 300 mammal species, 977 bird species, 176 reptiles, 105 amphibians, and 269 freshwater fish. Of the 10,000 plant species, about 3,160 are endemic. The Eastern Himalayas are a biodiversity hotspot that includes parts of Nepal, Bhutan, Sikkim, Arunanchal Pradesh, and Burma. This region has an estimated 9,000 plant species, with 3,500 endemic.
