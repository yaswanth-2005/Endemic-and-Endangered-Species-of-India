About Endangered and Endemic Species

What are Endangered Species?

Endangered species are those that are at risk of becoming extinct. This can be due to various factors such as habitat loss, climate change, pollution, and human activities. Conservation efforts are crucial to prevent the extinction of these species.

What are Endemic Species?

Endemic species are native to a specific geographic area and are not naturally found anywhere else. These species are often unique and adapted to the local environment. Conservation of endemic species is important to preserve biodiversity in specific regions.
